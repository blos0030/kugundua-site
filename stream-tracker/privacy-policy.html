Privacy Policy

Kugundua LLC built the Stream Tracker app as a Freemium app. This SERVICE is provided by Kugundua LLC at no cost and is intended for use as is. Users have the option to purchase an "Ad-Free" upgrade via In-App Purchase.

1. Information Collection and Use

Stream Tracker is designed to be 100% anonymous. We do not require account registration, and we do not collect your name, email, or contact information. Your watchlist data and preferences are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

However, the app uses third-party SDKs that may collect information used to identify your device for advertising and analytics. These include:

  • Google Play Services & AdMob: Used to serve advertisements. These services may access your advertising ID (IDFA/AAID) to provide personalized ads. View Google's Policy.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: Used to process payments for the "Ad-Free" upgrade. View Apple's Policy.

2. Payments & Financial Data

When you make an In-App Purchase, the transaction is handled entirely by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Kugundua LLC does not receive or store your credit card numbers or banking information. We only receive a "receipt" token from the store to confirm your "Ad-Free" status.

3. Tracking & Opt-Out (ATT)

On iOS devices, we respect Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. The app will ask for your permission before tracking your activity across other companies' apps and websites. You can change your selection at any time in your device Settings under "Privacy & Security."

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Because your data is stored locally, you have total control. You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Clearing the app cache/data in your device settings.
  • Uninstalling the app from your device.

5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Regardless of your location, we respect your rights to access, correct, or delete any data we might have. Since we do not collect personal identifiers like emails, we generally cannot link a request to a specific user. However, if you have questions regarding your data rights, please contact us.
